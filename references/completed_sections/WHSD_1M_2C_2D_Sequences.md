### 1.4 Opener's Action After Responder's Invitational Rebid

These follow from natural logic and negative inference (anything "interesting" would have been shown as a puppet break). No special agreements needed ‚Äî general principles apply:

- **Accept** by raising or bidding game
- **Decline** by passing
- **New suit** = bad canape, rejecting the suggested strain. (A good canape would have broken the puppet.)
- **Re-invite** = marginal accept, giving responder a last chance

## General Principles Referenced
