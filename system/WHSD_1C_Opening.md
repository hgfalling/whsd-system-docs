# WHSD: 1♣ Opening (1st and 2nd Seat)

---

## Opening Requirements

**11+ HCP**, bounded above by 1♦ (17+ unbalanced, 18+ balanced) and below by 1M (8–11 with a 4+ card major).

Contains hands in the following categories:

- **Balanced, 15–17 HCP.** Systemically includes balanced hands with a 5-card major.
- **Single major, good 15 to bad 17.** Semi-balanced or unbalanced with 5+M and 3 or fewer cards in the other major — too good for 2M but not good enough for 1♦.
- **Single long minor, 11–16.** 6+ cards in one minor, 3 or fewer in the other.
- **Minor-major, 11–16.** 5+ card minor with a 4-card major, unbalanced.
- **Oddballs, 11+.** 4441, 5440, and any other hands with no other home in the system.

---

## Uncontested Responses

| Response | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **Pass** | Rare. Should promise clubs. |
| **1♦** | Artificial waiting bid. Mostly negative inferences: cannot be 8+ with a 4cM, cannot be 8+ balanced. Unlimited. |
| **1♥** | 4+ hearts, 8+ HCP. Bid 1♥ with 4-4 in the majors. With 5-5, bid 1♠ with less than invitational strength, otherwise start with 1♥. |
| **1♠** | 4+ spades, 8+ HCP. With 4-4 majors, bid 1♥ instead. With 5♠–4♥, bid 1♠. |
| **1NT** | 8–10 balanced, denies a 4-card major. |
| **2♣** | Both majors, 5-4+, 6–11 HCP. |
| **2♦/2♥/2♠** | Preemptive, 6+ cards, 3–7 HCP. |
| **2NT** | 11–12 balanced, denies a 4-card major. |
| **3NT** | To play. 13–15 balanced, or any hand that wants to declare 3NT opposite a 1♣ opener. Opener should generally pass even with 17 unless holding a good 6+ minor looking for 6m or 6NT. |
| **3♣–3♠** | Preemptive. |
| **4♣/4♦** | Namyats (long major, 5–5.5 losers). |
| **4NT** | Pick a minor. |

With minor-suit hands below game force, responder bids 1♦ and sorts it out later, or bids NT if practical.
