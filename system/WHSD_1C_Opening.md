## Opening Requirements

**11+ HCP**, bounded above by 1‚ô¶ (17+ unbalanced, 18+ balanced) and below by 1M (8‚Äì11 with a 4+ card major).

Contains hands in the following categories:

- **Balanced, 15‚Äì17 HCP.** Systemically includes balanced hands with a 5-card major.
- **Single major, good 15 to bad 17.** Semi-balanced or unbalanced with 5+M and 3 or fewer cards in the other major ‚Äî too good for 2M but not good enough for 1‚ô¶.
- **Single long minor, 11‚Äì16.** 6+ cards in one minor, 3 or fewer in the other.
- **Minor-major, 11‚Äì16.** 5+ card minor with a 4-card major, unbalanced.
- **Oddballs, 11+.** 4441, 5440, and any other hands with no other home in the system.
